Seen a lot on stackoverflow (and elsewhere): "Don't reinvent the wheel".


"Si on ne réinvente pas la roue, on ne peut pas l'améliorer"

If you reinvent a concept, you can understand it better.

How can you improve something if you don't understand how it works?
