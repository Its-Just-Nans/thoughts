> From 2021-09-07 to 2024-08-31

One of France's top engineering schools in the Grandes Ecoles system.
Equivalent to a top-tier university in the UK/US.


- 1st year
    - general engineering
- 2nd year
    - major in *TELECOM* (antennas and telecommunications)
    - in apprenticeship at Quandela https://www.quandela.com/
- 3rd year
    - major in *RIO* (networks and IoT)
    - in apprenticeship at Quandela https://www.quandela.com/
